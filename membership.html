Membership Description

Role of the members

To assist and motivate all areas of the Church in a manner that will result to the advancement of the kingdom.
Major focus will be placed to encouraging New comers, involvement and assimilating new members.
Also working with to mobilize our church toward the Mission and Vision.
Focusing on to Assist the Pastor will bring a sense of belonging, trust, accountability and responsibility to each member of our assembly.






Learn more

Qualification

1. Must meet the qualifications and requirements for the church service.
2. Must be loyal to the Pastor and this short and long-term visions.
3. Must be willing to work in harmony with others.
4. Must be faithful and dependable in accomplishing duties.
5. Must carry love and burden for the souls of men, women and children.
6. Must lead, encourage, assimilate and care for your group with tact, zeal and love.
7. Must be flexible and willing to do whatever it takes to get the job done.
8. Must be given to prayer in your personal life and for your group of members.
9. Must possess a general Biblical knowledge ,e.g Coming to Bible Study, and others activities in the church
10. Must strive to retain love for your of ministry.

Learn more

Responsibilities

1. Shall seek to fulfill all responsibilities with dedication and love to work and respect for all members.
2. Shall maintain a weekly contact with the Ministers and , working together to communicate with others members as often as necessary.
3. Shall follow-up on all responsibilities delegated to you by the Ministers.
4. Shall maintain a positive attitude in all that you do.
5. Promote Faithfulness in Tithe, Offering and other area giving Opportunities
6. Promote Faithfulness in Church Attendance and Informing When Absent
7. Shall consistently affirm God’s work in their lives.
8. Ask the New members “What is needed be done in their life/home/family that we can add to our prayer list?
9. If they choose not to share a need, let them know that you will be placing their family name on your prayer list.
10. Shall communicate with the Ministers concerning special situations. Please use your judgement and much discretion.
11. Never violate a confidence from one member to another member.
12. Pray with members that are troubled or discouraged.

Other duties

1. Shall be an example to the Church in soul winning by continually being involved in the outreach ministry of your choice, with Pastor's approval.
2. Shall be an example to the Church in spiritual growth by coming at least 30 minutes early to pray before each service.
3. Shall notify Minister in advance if unable to attend a service or function for any reason.
4. Shall remember that your service to God goes beyond the church service and building. Your actions outside the church also dictate people’s response to the effectiveness of the mission.
5. Shall perform additional duties as required.

Relationship

All members are directly accountable to the Ministers. Each year Pastor will review this ministry description for updates and improvements to make it more applicable to members. Accountability shall consist of meeting attendance Pastor will perform evaluation of performance of Members
Workers and Disciples training will be held every 30mn before our Tuesday and Thursday weekly meeting. This training is to prepare and equip those who have the desire to work in the house of God or in the Ministry. This is very important . Also beginners class will start , for the new convert, to know the fundamental truth of the bible
